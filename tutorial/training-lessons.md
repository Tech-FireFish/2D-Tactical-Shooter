# Training Lesson Notes

Playable tutorial levels live in `tutorials/` and appear in the Tutorial selector inside Mission Setup.

- Basics Movement teaches WASD, sneaking, sprinting, and VIP completion.
- Shooting Modes teaches automatic shooting, manual hold-to-fire, and respawning practice targets.
- Operators And Stairs teaches operator selection and stair transfers.
- Equipment Table starts the operator with no weapon and no armor, then teaches table loadouts.
- Windows teaches hand-opening, shooting glass, bullet pass-through, and vaulting.
- Digital Lock teaches paper clues, inventory, keypad entry, unlocking, then opening the door.
