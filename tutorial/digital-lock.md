# Digital Lock Tutorial

This tutorial is implemented as the playable level `level/tutorial-digital-lock.json`.

Walkthrough:

1. Move the operator near the yellow paper clue.
2. Press `E` to pick up the paper.
3. Press `Tab` to open inventory and read the generated door code.
4. Move near the digital lock door.
5. Press `E` to open the keypad.
6. Enter the paper code by clicking keypad numbers or typing digits.
7. Press Unlock.
8. Press `E` again to open the unlocked door.
9. Move to the VIP/objective marker.

Important behavior:

- Digital door passwords are randomized on level restart.
- Paper clue text is updated to match the generated password.
- Unlocking a digital door keeps it closed.
- The player must interact with the unlocked door a second time to open it.
